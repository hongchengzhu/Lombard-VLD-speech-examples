Speech examples of DB-MMLC

Anonymous Authors

This page gives several groups of speech samples from our self-made DB-MMLC (Multi-factor Mandarin Lombard corpus), so that reviewers can more directly and intuitively feel the acoustic differences of live speakers caused by Lombard effect.

Note that the contents of DB-MMLC are the Grid-like Mandarin sentences that are consisted of five components, i.e., name, verb, classifier, adjective, and noun in order. Each of these components is provided with 20 phoneme-balanced candidate words and they are selected randomly to construct sentences. The randomly generated sentences have no actual meanings. More details are available in Appendix A in our paper.
